Shahi Paneer

Recipe

Description: Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a luxurious sauce of tomatoes, onions, and cashew nuts, flavored with aromatic spices. It’s a royal dish, often served at special occasions.

Ingredients:

  1. Paneer
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Onions
  4. Cashew Nuts
  5. Cream
  6. Ginger Garlic Paste
  7. Green cardamom
  8. Bayleaf
  9. Cumin seeds
  10. Coriander powder
  11. Garam masala
  12. Red chili powder
  13. Salt
  14. Oil or ghee (for cooking)
  15. Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds, green cardamom, and bay leaf. Sauté until aromatic. Add onions and ginger-garlic paste. Cook until golden.
  • Stir in pureed tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  • Add the cashew paste to the mixture and cook for a few minutes.
  • Add paneer cubes and gently stir to coat them in the gravy.
  • Add cream and garam masala, and simmer for a couple of minutes. Adjust the consistency with water if needed.
  • Serve Hot: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with naan or rice.
