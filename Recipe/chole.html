Chole Bhature

Recipe

Description: Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish, consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with fluffy, deep-fried bread (bhature). It's a hearty and flavorful meal, often enjoyed for breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients:

  1. Boiled chickpeas
  2. Onions (finely chopped)
  3. Tomatoes (pureed)
  4. Ginger-garlic paste
  5. Green chilies
  6. Cumin seeds
  7. Coriander powder
  8. Red chili powder
  9. Garam masala
  10. Chole masala (optional)
  11. Salt
  12. Oil
  13. Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  14. All-purpose flour (maida)
  15. Yogurt
  16. Baking powder
  17. Salt
  18. Water (for kneading)
  19. Oil (for frying)
